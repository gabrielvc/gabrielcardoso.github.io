Gabriel V. Cardoso


Semantics Scholar / Github / Email

I am currently a PhD candidate at CMAP (Ecole Polytechnique) and the Institut Liryc, where I work with Eric Moulines and Rémi Dubois. Prior to this, I was a Machine Learning Engineer at Volta Medical.

My PhD research focuses on Generative Models, with a particular interest in applications to Cardiology and general health data. Recently, I have been working with diffusion models and their usage as a prior for solving ill-posed inverse problems.
