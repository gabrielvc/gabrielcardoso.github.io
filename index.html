I am currently a PhD candidate at CMAP (Ecole polytechnique) and at Institut Liryc working with Eric Moulines and Rémi Dubois. Previously I was a Machine learning Engineer at Volta Medical.

My PhD focus on Generative models, with a particular interest in applications to Cardiology and in general health data. Recently, I've been working with diffusion models and their usage as prior for inverse problems.
